Welcome to Global Street Team

Our Mission

The mission of Global Worship Radio Street Team is to bring the heart of Global Worship Radio into communities through meaningful, Spirit-led interactions. We are committed to connecting people to Jesus by sharing His love through prayer, the Word of God, and worship experiences that inspire hope, faith, and transformation.

As a team, we seek to create genuine personal connections that extend beyond the radio and into everyday life. Through outreach, encouragement, and acts of service, we aim to build a welcoming community where people can encounter God’s presence and grow in their relationship with Him.

Frequently Asked Questions

It is a community of trained volunteers who serve through outreach, prayer, media, and events to bring worship and the message of Jesus to cities locally and globally.
No experience is required. We provide structured online training to equip you for your specific role, whether in outreach, leadership, media, or prayer.
After signing up, you will go through online training based on your selected role. Once completed, you can join or start a local team in your area.
Commitment varies depending on your availability and role, but we encourage consistency, teamwork, and participation in outreach activities and team communication.
